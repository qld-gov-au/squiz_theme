Weather conditions codes are:
	
	100: Sunny (Clear at night)
	101: Mostly sunny (Mostly clear at night)
	200: Cloudy
	208: Shower /storms
	220: Cloudy /showers (seems to correlate with pop ~70%)
	221: Cloudy /showers (seems to correlate with pop ~40%. i.e: A little less rainy than 220)
	
	
	Others (unknown codes)
		Sunny /windy
		Foggy
		Late storms
		Early /sandstorm
		Cloudy /snow
		Rain /snow
		Rain
		Snow
